# Asset Manifest

This prototype ships with procedural assets. The playable objects are generated in `src/main.js` with Three.js primitives.

## Included visual assets

- `concept.svg`, promotional concept image.
- Player: capsule body, head, fists, dynamic shadow.
- Goblin kid: smaller capsule body, green head, nose cone, ears and fists.
- Enemy variants: fast goblin, tank goblin and boss goblin.
- Colosseum: cylinder arena, stone wall, upper ring, repeated columns and banners.
- Combat FX: procedural hit particles and floating damage text.
- UI: CSS panels, HUD and reward cards.

## External dependency

- Three.js module from `https://unpkg.com/three@0.164.1/build/three.module.js`.
